The wait is over! tBG37 kicks off today!

the BIG GAME crew August 8, 2014

Thats right gamers, tBG37 is today! (doors open at 18:00). 

If your still at a loss as to what to bring to the event head on over to our FAQs and check the list for some helpfull hints. The venue address details are also available further down this article.

We look forward to seeing you there!

Under 16? if your under 16 we'll need to you bring the parental consent form with you to the event, either complete it and email it in to us or print it out and bring it along with you to the event. Simples!!

Venue Address details:

Wildern School,
Wildern Lane,
Hedge End,
Southampton,
Hampshire
SO30 4EJ

Any problems, questions or queries getting to the event or about the event its self, ping us an email or tweet us (Dont forget to use the hashtag #tBG37)
