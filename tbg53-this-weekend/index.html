tBG53: THIS WEEKEND!

the BIG GAME crew June 30, 2025

The countdown to the BIG GAME is officially on, and we're buzzing with excitement to see you all this coming weekend! Get ready for an epic weekend of gaming, good food, and great company.

Here's some crucial info to ensure you're all set for the event:

Camping at the BIG GAME

Planning to pitch a tent and stay onsite? Awesome! Please make sure you register for camping via the "Manage Your Booking" section on our Ticket Tailor page. Registering helps us get an accurate headcount and ensures there's plenty of space for everyone.

Door Open Times & New Venue

Mark your calendars! Doors open at 18:00 on Friday, July 4th. If you arrive a bit early, you can access the cafe for refreshments and snacks starting from 17:00 while you wait.

Please note our NEW VENUE ADDRESS: Havant RFC, PO9 3EJ - Make sure you head to the right place!

Friday Night Curry: Don't Miss Out!

Forget about cooking on Friday night and join us for our delicious Friday night curry! For just £12 per person, you'll get a mild chicken (or vegetable) curry, fluffy rice, crispy poppadoms, and a warm, soft naan. It's the perfect way to fuel up for a weekend of gaming without any hassle.

To ensure we have enough food for everyone, please pre-order your curry within the next 24 hours via this link.

And for those wondering about Saturday, we'll also have pizza available to keep you fueled!

Quick Reminder of the Ground Rules

To ensure everyone has a fantastic and safe time, here are a couple of important reminders:

  • Be excellent to each other! Let's keep the atmosphere friendly and respectful for all attendees.
  • The venue is a licensed premises, which means NO external alcohol is permitted. Please respect this rule.

We can't wait to see you all for the BIG GAME! Get your rigs ready and prepare for an unforgettable weekend.

See you there!
