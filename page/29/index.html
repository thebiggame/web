Food Storage

We have access to several super-size fridges at the event - allowing you to store your food and beverages keeping them chilled and fresh, we do ask that you don't overload the fridges though and please stack your food and drink responsibly. Hot Water, Tea, coffee, milk, sugar and fresh chilled water as well as cutlery are available for free for the duration of the event. We do ask that you please try and keep the food storage and preparation area as clean as possible and clean up after yourself if you a mess.
