additional front page info

the BIG GAME crew January 28, 2014

At the BIG GAME we aim to combine individual and team tournament play with a relaxed and friendly atmosphere over the weekend. The main focus of the event is the BIG GAMER tournament which is an event long multi discipline tournament, our aim is to find the best all round gamers at the events, gamers earn points for each tournament they play and place in them, the tournament culminates in the top scoring gamers over the weekend taking part in a finale on Sunday to find the overall BIG GAMER! who will claim not only the title of BIG GAMER, the much prized Golden Joystick (which gains them FREE entry to the next event!) but also the prizes from our awesome event sponsors!
