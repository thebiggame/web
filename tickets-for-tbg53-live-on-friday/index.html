Tickets for tBG53 - Live on Friday!

the BIG GAME crew October 8, 2024

We're thrilled to announce that tickets for BIG GAME 53, our 53rd annual LAN party, will go on sale this Friday, October 11th. This year's event promises to be bigger and better than ever, with a packed schedule of tournaments, competitions, and non-stop gaming fun.

Get your tickets here

What to expect at BIG GAME 53:

Epic tournaments: Compete against the best gamers for bragging rights, BIG GAMER title and prizes.

Non-stop gaming: Enjoy a variety of games, from classic titles to the latest releases.

Social atmosphere: Connect with fellow gamers, make new friends, and share your passion.

Food and drinks: Fuel up with delicious food and refreshing drinks throughout the event.

Don't miss out, be part of gaming history. Mark your calendars and get ready to grab your tickets when they go live on Friday, October 11th.

Stay tuned for more updates on BIG GAME 53!
