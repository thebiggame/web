Mark One Levels Up! They're Back for The BIG GAME 2025!

the BIG GAME crew October 3, 2024

Get ready to frag, farm, and dominate, because the BIG GAME is gearing up for another epic year of LAN party action, and we're thrilled to announce that our awesome sponsor, Mark One, is joining the fray once again!

That's right, folks, Mark One (https://markone.co.uk/) continues their long-standing support of the BIG GAME in to 2025! Their continued support means that gamers of all levels can expect top-notch equipment, support, incredible prizes, and an unforgettable weekend of competitive fun.

A Huge Thank You to Mark One

We at The BIG GAME want to extend a massive thank you to the team at Mark One. Their dedication to the gaming community is truly inspiring, and their support allows us to create a truly special experience for all attendees.
