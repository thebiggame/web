tBG52... It's ON like donkey kong!

the BIG GAME crew April 15, 2024

People keep asking if we're back... I'm thinking we're back! and like a phoenix from the ashes the BIG GAME has a new look AND an all-new location!

Running from 10am on Saturday the 1st of June 2024 to 6pm Sunday our next event will offer 64 gamers 32 hours of social and competitive gaming at our new home, Havant RFC.

BOOK YOUR TICKETS!

We're setting a high bar for our comeback with new event features and the return of your favourites, including:

  • Dedicated on-site pub with space for board games.
  • Plush conference seating (no more plastic folding chairs).
  • On-site catering including cooked breakfast, tea, and coffee.
  • the BIG GAME Pizza run!
  • the BIG GAMER Tournament (multi-discipline event long tournament).
  • Sleeping area away from the main gaming hall.
  • Multiple screens around the venue to keep you abreast of the action.
  • Dedicated dining area.

Tickets for the event are £50 per gamer and we are continuing to offer our clan discount, book 5 or more tickets at one time to receive a 20% discount on the whole order!

Tickets will be going on general sale at 8 pm on Thursday the 18th of April, if you register for early access you will receive a code via email to access the tickets from 6pm. If you haven’t received your early access code by noon on Thursday, contact the crew on discord!

We are using a new ticket platform for this event so if you intend to use the clan discount please make sure you’ve viewed the guide! viewed the guide video
