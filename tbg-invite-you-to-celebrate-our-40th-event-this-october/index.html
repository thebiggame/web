tBG invite you to celebrate our 40th event this October

the BIG GAME crew July 30, 2015

This October, the BIG GAME celebrates a very special milestone in its history, our 40th BIG GAME LAN party event! Running over the weekend of October 30th. the BIG GAME will again host its legendary BIG GAMER tournament where we pit every gamer against the other in an event long league to find the best gamer of the event and crown them BIG GAMER 40!

With that gamer taking home the raft of awesomely assembled prizes - thanks to our sponsors Novatech, who have again put together an awesome selection of top-tier tech up for grabs. As well as, of course, the prize whose value is immeasurable, the title of BIG GAMER 40 and the BIG GAMER Golden Joystick its self!

We look forward to seeing you at tBG40 this October :)
