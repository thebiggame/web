tBG52 dates announced for end of May

the BIG GAME crew January 17, 2020

We are exciting an announce, the 52nd running of the BIG GAME LAN party series will take place on 29th - 31st of May 2020.

Ticket will be available from 2 weeks today, on the 31st of January at 18:00, so there is plenty of time to organise your friends and clanmates to ensure you can put your strongest team forward for the ever-competitive (and unique) BIG GAMER tourney and to be in with a chance to win the awesome prize bundles assembled by our fantastic sponsors Novatech.

We can't wait for tBG52 to kick off and look forward to welcoming you for some early summer LAN party gaming and antics!
