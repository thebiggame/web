tBG52 - A Roaring Success!

the BIG GAME crew June 2, 2024

Wow, what a weekend! Our 52nd LAN party at our ALL-NEW location, Havant Rugby Club, has officially wrapped, and it's safe to say it was an absolute blast! The new location was fantastic, with tons of space and facilities for everyone to set up their rigs and frag out in comfort as well as plentiful stocks of food and drink too. Whether you were dominating the leaderboards or just chilling with friends, the atmosphere was electric throughout so thanks to everyone for that.

A huge thank you to everyone who came out as well as all the crew and helpers (from cabling help through to lighting and tech support) that came and setup the event and made this event so special. It wouldn't have been the same without your enthusiasm and friendly competition. Special congratulations goes out to "Thatbloke" for taking home the coveted title of "BIG GAMER Champion" this year! We were all incredibly impressed with your skills and how quickly you picked up the BIG GAMER finale game too - truly a pro.

All the pictures from the event will be online soon.

We'll wrap things up by thanking you all once again and our FRAG'tastic sponsors, Mark One Consultants and Yayzi Broadband for their continued support of the event.

We're already buzzing with fresh ideas for the next LAN party, and we can't wait to see you all again soon. Stay tuned for updates and announcements!
