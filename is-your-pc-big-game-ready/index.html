Is your PC BIG GAME ready?

the BIG GAME crew January 31, 2015

Make sure your PC is up-to-date and BIG GAME ready with the latest hardware to get your favorite FPS shooters running smoothly and gaming peripherals to improve your aim and sounds, with a vast inventory kept in-stock and at the very best prices, head over to our friends at Novatech for all your tech needs.

Located in Portsmouth (PO6 4PU) its just a short hop from the BIG GAME venue and with a vast inventory of the latest gaming gear and a wealth of knowledge on hand to fix any PC problems you might have, Novatech has everything you need to get your PC BIG GAME ready in time for the LAN!
