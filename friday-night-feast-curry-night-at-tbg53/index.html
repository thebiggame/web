Friday Night Feast: Curry Night at tBG53!

the BIG GAME crew June 13, 2025

Get ready, tBG53 attendees, because we're kicking off our 53rd event with a delicious new tradition! This Friday night (July 4th), we're inviting everyone to join us for a fantastic curry feast right here at the tBG53.

After you've set up your rig and started getting into the gaming zone, you won't need to worry about dinner. We're serving up a spread that's sure to satisfy those pre-gaming hunger pangs!

For just £12 per person, you'll get:

  • Your choice of chicken curry or a delicious vegetable curry
  • Fluffy rice
  • Crispy poppadoms
  • Warm and soft Naan breads
  • A selection of tasty sauces

We'll make sure there's more than enough for everyone to enjoy a bit of everything. It's the perfect way to fuel up for a weekend of intense gaming and great company.

You can order your Friday night curry here <----
(Curry orders close on Friday 29th June)

So, get ready to game, but first, get ready to feast! We can't wait to see you there!
